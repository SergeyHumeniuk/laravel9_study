    Ride above the ground with the Hoversurf S3 eVTOL Hoverbike Drone. This single personal drone makes it possible to fly without a pilot license. Offering complete freedom of flight, the eVTOL Hoverbike Drone works with an app to help you navigate in the air. Likewise, it prevents you from flying in restricted flight zones and interfering with others. Made of different kinds of carbon, the monocoque frame is a single element that is lighter than the previous model. Moreover, the eVTOL Hoverbike Drone reaches up to 60 miles per hour. Additionally, the hoverbike design enables it to be rolled through a regular doorway. It can also take off and land from your average parking space. The eVTOL Hoverbike Drone offers up to 40 minutes of flight time in drone mode. With a pilot, the hoverbike works up to 25 minutes, depending on the flight condition and weight.
